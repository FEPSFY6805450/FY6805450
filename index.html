Introduction

   

    

 Biodiversity consists of a variety of species such as plants, animals and different micro organisms. (National Geographic, n.d.).
One of the biggest threats to the planet includes greenhouse gases. These are gases that absorb infrared radiation emitted from the earth’s surface (Mann, 2019). Global warming is also hazardous to biodiversity. The issue of greenhouse gases and global warming is rapidly increasing.
Figures show that the average temperature globally has increased from 0.40 degrees to 0.74 degrees. I chose this topic because I wanted to reflect upon the danger that biodiversity faces every single day.
